TOPIC N 2 :



ONE OF THE FUNCTIONS OF ART IS TO CHALLENGE THE ESTABLISHMENT. THERE SHOULD THEREFORE BE NO RESTRICTIONS IN THE FORM OF CENSORSHIP ON ARTISTS. TO WHAT EXTENT DO YOU AGREE OR DISAGREE?


Parrafo 1:

IT IS FAIR TO SAY THAT ART IS PRECISELY A JOURNEY TO EXPRESS OUR CREATIVITY RATHER THAN A FIXED CONCEPT SO IT SHOULD NEITHER BE JUDGED NOR LABELED. IT IS MY BELIEF THAT TRYING TO SET RULES SO AS TO CONCEPTUALIZE ART CAN BE A TRICKY JOB BUT THERE IS NOTHING WRONG WITH PURSUING WAYS TO STRUCTURE IT ACADEMICALLY.

 

Parrafo 2:

THE TRUTH BE TOLD, ART IS AN ETHEREAL CONCEPT, FULL OF INTRICACIES AND COMPLEXITIES, DOWNRIGHT CONTROVERSIAL SO TRYING TO DRAW THE LINE IN TERMS OF WHETHER ‚ÄòTHIS‚Äô OR ‚ÄòTHAT' WORK OF ART IS EITHER BETTER OR WORSE IS POINTLESS. FOR INSTANCE, GRAFFITI IS JUDGED BY MOST EDUCATED PAINTERS AND USUALLY CALLED ‚ÄòA FORM OF VANDALISM‚Äô YET, I HAVE SEEN SOME OF THE MOST EXTRAORDINARY TALENTS ON THE STREETS OF AMSTERDAM (MUCH OF WHICH, SEVERAL ‚ÄòFORMAL, ABSTRACT PAINTERS‚Äô WISH THEY HAD). THUS, WHEN IT COMES TO PAINTING FOR EXAMPLE, THE VISUAL NATURE OF SUCH ART MIGHT GIVE SOME KIND OF AUTHORITY TO ‚ÄòTHE EDUCATED EYE‚Äô TO JUDGE ART AS SUCH, YET, ART IS BEYOND HUMAN JUDGEMENT.   

 

Parrafo 3:

HOWEVER, THE FACT THAT ART IS HARD TO DEFINE BY NATURE MEANS THAT IT IS ALSO FREE TO STUDY, PERFORM AND EVEN TRY TO ESTABLISH PROCESSES FOR ITS UNDERSTANDING, PERFORMANCE AND APPRECIATION SO THE VOICE OF ART CRITICS SHOULD ALSO BE HEARD. ART ACADEMICS SAY THAT THERE ARE TECHNIQUES AND BASIC KNOWLEDGE TO BE ACQUIRED PRIOR TO PERFORMING CLASSICAL PIECES OF MUSIC AND THAT (REGARDLESS OF THEIR TALENT) PIANO PLAYERS NEED TO COMPLY WITH CERTAIN FUNDAMENTALS TO PERFORM ART 'THE RIGHT WAY' AND EARN RESPECT AND I  BELIEVE THAT TRYING TO ORGANIZE A WAY TO CULTIVATE ART IS NOBLE. THEN AGAIN, SUCH CONCEPT IS ONCE AGAIN CHALLENGED WHEN WE SEE GENIUSES YOUNGER THAN TEN, BEING ABLE TO DEFY THE ESTABLISHMENT AND SHOW THE WORLD THAT THE GIFT FOR ART ALSO COMES GENETICALLY AND THEREFORE, NO EDUCATION OR THEORY IS NEEDED.

 


Parrafo 4:

IN SUM, EVEN THOUGH ART CAN BE UNDERSTOOD TO SOME DEGREE, IT CANNOT BE FULLY COMPREHENDED GIVEN THAT THEORETICAL, HUMAN KNOWLEDGE CAN ONLY GO SO FAR WHEREAS ART HAS EVEN BEEN DEFINED AS ‚ÄòDIVINE‚Äô OR EVEN ‚ÄòNOT FROM THIS EARTH' SO WE SHOULD THINK OUT OF THE BOX WHEN IT COMES TO ART.

Parrafo 2:

Parrafo 3:

LIKEWISE, ANOTHER EFFECT OF CONSUMERISM THAT COULD BE CONSIDERED ‚ÄòPOSITIVE‚Äô IS THAT INDUSTRIES ARE MANUFACTURING NEW PRODUCTS WITH RE-USABLE MATERIALS AND A CULTURE OF RECYCLING MATERIALS IS NOW BEING PROMOTED ALL AROUND THE WORLD DUE TO THE FACT THAT MORE PEOPLE ARE BUYING MORE THINGS OUT OF CONSUMERISM. TO ILLUSTRATE, LET‚ÄôS TAKE THE CAR INDUSTRY FOR INSTANCE: OUR CARS ARE NOT MADE FROM IRON ANYMORE, OR OTHER DURABLE MATERIALS AND PEOPLE ARE BUYING MORE CARS SOMETIMES MORE OUT OF PEER PRESSURE THAN ANYTHING ELSE BUT ALONG WITH SUCH CONSUMERISM, THERE IS A CULTURE OF RECYCLE IN THE WORLD AND IT IS ESTIMATED THAT IN 20 YEARS, OUR CARS WILL HAVE ONLY A 10% OF HEAVY MATERIALS. THEN AGAIN, CONSUMERISM CAN HAVE A FEW BENEFICIAL SIDES YET, WE MUST BEWARE OF THE FACT THAT IT  FOSTERS A SENSE OF EMPTINESS IN US ALL AND IN TURN, MAKES US SUPERFICIAL.


Parrafo 4:

IN A NUTSHELL, ALTHOUGH CONSUMERISM SHOULD NOT BE CONSIDERED AS A POSITIVE SOCIAL TREND, IT DOES HAVE SOME EFFECTS THAT ARE PART OF OUR ECONOMIC GROWTH AND BEHAVIOR WORLWIDE. NEVERTHELESS, WE MUST REFLECT ON THE CONSEQUENCES OF EXCEEDING OUR BUDGETS.
